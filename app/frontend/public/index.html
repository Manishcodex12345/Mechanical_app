Invoice Approval Workflow

A visual demo of the request-to-approval flow for material invoices shipped from India to USA/Canada.

Workflow Steps

  1. 1) Submit Request

    User submits invoice number, material, weight, cost, destination, ship date, and priority.

  2. 2) Validation

    System checks mandatory fields and validates weight/cost/date before routing.

  3. 3) HR Admin Queue

    Requests appear in pending queue sorted by Priority (High ‚Üí Low) and oldest first.

  4. 4) Decision

    HR Admin can Approve, Reject (with reason), or ask for Clarification.

  5. 5) Notify & Ship

    Notifications are sent and approved requests move to Ready-to-Ship / Dispatched.

Priority SLA Matrix

High Priority

Reminder: 1 hour

Escalation: 4 hours

Medium Priority

Reminder: 6 hours

Escalation: 24 hours

Low Priority

Reminder: 24 hours

Escalation: 48 hours

Notification Channels

In-App Email Teams / Slack (optional) SMS / WhatsApp for escalations

Roles & Responsibility

Requester

  • Creates invoice approval request.
  • Sets accurate priority.
  • Tracks status and responds to clarifications.

HR Admin

  • Reviews pending requests by priority SLA.
  • Approves / rejects / asks clarification.
  • Ensures audit-ready decision trail.

Manager (Escalation)

  • Handles SLA breaches.
  • Monitors turnaround and bottlenecks.
  • Improves policy and decision consistency.
